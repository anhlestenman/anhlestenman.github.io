Explore Saigon's Iconic Landmarks and Hidden Gems

Discover the vibrant tapestry of culture, history, and modernity that awaits you in Saigon. Here are some must-visit destinations that capture the essence of this dynamic city:

Ben Thanh Market

Immerse yourself in the hustle and bustle of Saigon's most famous market, where you can shop for local handicrafts, textiles, souvenirs, and fresh produce. Sample delicious street food and immerse yourself in the vibrant atmosphere of this iconic market, located in the heart of District 1.

Notre-Dame Cathedral Basilica of Saigon

Marvel at the striking red-brick facade and towering spires of this historic Catholic cathedral, a symbol of French colonial architecture in Saigon. Located in District 1, this iconic landmark offers a peaceful retreat from the city's bustling streets.

War Remnants Museum

Gain profound insights into Vietnam's tumultuous past and the effects of the Vietnam War at the War Remnants Museum. Explore powerful exhibits documenting the country's struggle for independence and reconciliation with its past atrocities.

Bitexco Financial Tower Sky Deck

Soar to new heights and enjoy panoramic views of Saigon from the observation deck of the Bitexco Financial Tower, one of the city's tallest skyscrapers. Experience breathtaking vistas of the Saigon River, city skyline, and surrounding landmarks.

Saigon Opera House

Step back in time and admire the grandeur of the Saigon Opera House, a stunning example of French colonial architecture. Attend a captivating performance or simply marvel at the building's elegant facade and ornate interiors.

Cu Chi Tunnels

Journey outside the city and explore the historic Cu Chi Tunnels, an intricate network of underground passages used by Viet Cong soldiers during the Vietnam War. Delve into the tunnels' fascinating history and gain insights into guerrilla warfare tactics.
