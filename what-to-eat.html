Indulge in Saigon's Culinary Delights

Prepare your taste buds for a culinary adventure unlike any other in Saigon, where the vibrant street food scene, traditional dishes, and innovative culinary creations await. Here's a taste of what the city has to offer:

Pho

Dive into the quintessential Vietnamese dish, pho, a fragrant noodle soup made with flavorful broth, rice noodles, and tender slices of beef or chicken. Sample this beloved comfort food at local pho stalls or renowned eateries like Pho Hoa and Pho 2000.

Banh Mi

Treat yourself to a Banh Mi, a delicious Vietnamese sandwich served on a crispy baguette and stuffed with savory fillings such as grilled pork, pate, pickled vegetables, and fresh herbs. Head to Banh Mi Huynh Hoa or Banh Mi 37 Nguyen Trai for some of the city's best sandwiches.

Bun Thit Nuong

Savor the vibrant flavors of Bun Thit Nuong, a refreshing Vietnamese noodle salad topped with grilled pork, fresh herbs, pickled vegetables, and crushed peanuts, served with a side of nuoc cham dipping sauce. Visit Bun Thit Nuong Chi Tuyen or Bun Thit Nuong Co Tam for a taste of perfection.

Com Tam

Feast on Com Tam, a hearty Vietnamese dish featuring broken rice served with grilled pork, pork skin, pickled vegetables, and a fried egg, drizzled with savory fish sauce. Delight in the flavors of Com Tam Ba Ghien or Com Tam Thuan Kieu for an authentic culinary experience.

Vietnamese Coffee

Indulge in the rich and aromatic flavors of Vietnamese coffee, brewed strong and served with sweetened condensed milk over ice. Sip on this delicious caffeinated beverage at local cafes like Cong Caphe or Trung Nguyen Coffee for a refreshing pick-me-up.
